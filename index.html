Dr. Luule Viilma

Healer, spiritual leader.

Dr. Luule Viilma was one of the most thoughtful people

Brief Biography

  • 1974 graduated the only university in Estonia with higher medical education
  • 1974-1991 practiced as an gynecologist in the local hospitals
  • 1991 opened private practice
  • 1992 attended two-week introductory course of parapsychology; creation of the holistic view to human health
  • 1994 the first book of "A Teaching of Survival" series was published
  • 1997 “A Teaching of Survival” in English was compiled
  • 1998 public opposition with the official medicine; official gynecologist license was taken away
  • 2002 caught in a car accident; died in a emergency car on a way to the local hospital
